BASSTHERMAL / privacy / website-image-inventory
BassThermal does not sell personal data. No BassThermal account is required. Starting URLs, discovered URLs and inventory data are processed locally by the application. The app must make network requests to user-selected public websites to perform scans. Those websites, hosting providers and normal network intermediaries may receive standard request information such as IP address and request metadata. The app is not designed to send the resulting inventory to a BassThermal backend. Microsoft Store may provide purchase, install or diagnostic information under Microsoft’s own policies. Contact: info@bassthermal.com.
