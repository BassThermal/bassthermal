Privacy Policy — Ring Snap
Effective date: June 16, 2026
Ring Snap is a one-tap arcade game published by BassThermal.
BassThermal does not sell personal data. Ring Snap does not require an account, does not collect names, email addresses, contacts, photos, precise location, camera data, microphone data, or user-uploaded content.
Local gameplay data
Ring Snap may store gameplay and preference data locally on your device, such as best score, gameplay stats, and ad/settings preferences. This data is used to run the game experience. It is not an online account and is not intentionally sent to BassThermal servers. You can remove local app data by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app.
Ads and third-party services
Ring Snap uses Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK to show banner and interstitial ads. Google and its advertising partners may collect or process data such as advertising identifiers, device and app information, IP address, approximate location inferred from IP address, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and performance or crash-related data. This data may be used for ad delivery, ad personalization where permitted, measurement, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.
Consent and privacy options
Where required, Ring Snap uses Google’s User Messaging Platform to request consent and provide privacy options for advertising-related data processing. Available choices may depend on your region and Google’s requirements.
Data sharing
BassThermal does not sell personal data. Data processed through Google AdMob is handled by Google and its partners according to their own policies and the choices available to you through Google, Android, and in-app consent/privacy controls.
Security
Ring Snap uses standard Android, WebView, and Google SDK mechanisms. Network communication used by advertising and consent services is handled through the relevant third-party SDKs. Ring Snap is not designed to transmit gameplay scores or personal files to BassThermal servers.
Retention and deletion
Local gameplay and settings data remain on your device until you clear the app’s data or uninstall the app. Ring Snap does not create user accounts, so there is no account deletion process. Data collected or processed by Google AdMob is retained according to Google’s policies and your Google/device privacy settings.
Contact
For privacy questions about Ring Snap, contact BassThermal at info@bassthermal.com.
