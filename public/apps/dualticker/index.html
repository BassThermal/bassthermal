overview
DualTicker is a live headline comparison tool. Put two news streams side by side, watch how stories move across sources, and quickly compare what different outlets are emphasizing.
workflow
  1. Choose or open a headline view.
  2. Watch two live news streams side by side.
  3. Compare source framing, story movement, and coverage emphasis in real time.
use cases
  • monitor breaking news from multiple angles
  • compare how different sources frame the same story
  • keep a live headline board open while working or streaming
  • scan political/news coverage without manually refreshing sites
who for
For streamers, researchers, politics watchers, news obsessives, and anyone who wants a live read on how different sources are covering the same moment.
