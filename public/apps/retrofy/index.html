RetroFy

Convert images into retro-style output with palette, scale, pixel, and dithering controls.

overview
RetroFy converts normal images into retro-style output using limited palettes, pixelated looks, and dithering effects. Use it for quick visual experiments, game-style assets, social posts, mockups, and old-screen aesthetics.
workflow
  1. Load an image.
  2. Choose a palette, scale, or dithering look.
  3. Export the converted retro-style result.
use cases
  • make retro-style social images
  • test pixel-art visual directions
  • create lo-fi mockup assets
  • process images without opening a full graphics editor
who for
For creators, game devs, designers, and anyone who wants quick retro image effects without setting up a full graphics workflow.
