overview
Coptic Dictionary is a compact lookup tool for searching English and Coptic terms, reviewing meanings, transliterations, forms, and saved vocabulary. It is built for study, reading, and quick reference rather than bloated language software.
workflow
  1. Search by English word, Coptic word, or transliteration.
  2. Review matching entries, meanings, forms, and related vocabulary.
  3. Save useful words for later study or vocabulary review.
use cases
  • look up English words in Coptic
  • identify Coptic words while reading
  • compare transliterations and forms
  • build a saved vocabulary list for study
who for
For Coptic learners, readers, researchers, church learners, and people working with Coptic vocabulary.
faq
Can I search English words too?
Yes. Coptic Dictionary supports lookup from English terms as well as Coptic terms and transliterations.
Does Coptic Dictionary require a BassThermal account?
No. Coptic Dictionary does not require a BassThermal account.
