BASSTHERMAL / DocBatch PDF Converter
DocBatch PDF Converter batch document conversion app
summary
DocBatch PDF Converter converts multiple DOCX, DOC, RTF, ODT, and TXT files into PDF locally on Windows, with configurable output locations, conversion options, and file-conflict handling.
workflow
  1. Add one or more supported document files.
  2. Choose the converter, output location, and conflict behavior.
  3. Run the batch and review the resulting PDF files.
use cases
  • convert multiple Word documents to PDF
  • process mixed DOCX, DOC, RTF, ODT, and TXT batches
  • keep document conversion on the local Windows computer
  • control output folders and existing-file behavior
who for
For office users, students, administrators, small businesses, and anyone who needs to convert groups of documents without uploading them one by one to an online service.
