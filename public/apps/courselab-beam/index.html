overview
CourseLab Beam is a focused Windows and Android calculator for building determinate beam-loading cases and viewing support reactions, shear-force diagrams, bending-moment diagrams, and calculation steps.
workflow
  1. Define the beam, supports, and geometry.
  2. Add point loads, distributed loads, or applied moments.
  3. Inspect reactions, diagrams, cursor values, and calculation steps.
use cases
  • study determinate beam behavior
  • check basic statics and structural-analysis exercises
  • compare how different loads affect shear and moment
  • review reactions and calculation steps on a Windows PC, phone, or tablet
who for
For civil and mechanical engineering students, structural-analysis learners, tutors, teachers, and anyone reviewing basic determinate beam cases.
faq
Does it require a BassThermal account?
No.
What is it for?
Educational and checking workflows for determinate vertical-load beams.
Is it a professional sealed-design application?
No. It is an educational and checking utility, not a sealed engineering-design tool.
Where is it installed?
From the Windows or Android link above.
