BASSTHERMAL / CourseLab Beam: Shear & Moment: shear & moment
CourseLab Beam shear force and bending moment calculator
summary
CourseLab Beam is a focused Android calculator for building determinate beam-loading cases and viewing support reactions, shear-force diagrams, bending-moment diagrams, and calculation steps.
workflow
  1. Define the beam, supports, and geometry.
  2. Add point loads, distributed loads, or applied moments.
  3. Inspect reactions, diagrams, cursor values, and calculation steps.
use cases
  • study determinate beam behavior
  • check basic statics and structural-analysis exercises
  • compare how different loads affect shear and moment
  • review reactions and calculation steps on a phone or tablet
who for
For civil and mechanical engineering students, structural-analysis learners, tutors, teachers, and anyone reviewing basic determinate beam cases.
